SERVICES

CONSTRUCTION

Founded in 1999 by Tomislav Djokic, Tennis Line Sol d.o.o. has established a robust presence in the sports court construction industry. Specializing in clay courts, hard courts, artificial grass, and all acrylic surfaces, we offer comprehensive solutions for building and restoring sports facilities. Operating across the Balkans and extending our services to Germany, Romania, and beyond, we have successfully constructed and restored over 1,000 tennis courts, showcasing our capability and reach.

Our most prestigious project was serving as the court staff for the Adria Tour in Zadar, Croatia, organized by the world-renowned tennis player Novak Djokovic. This opportunity not only underscored our commitment to excellence but also highlighted our ability to meet the stringent demands of professional sports events. At Tennis Line Sol d.o.o., we bring decades of expertise and a passion for sports to every project, ensuring top-quality results that stand the test of time.

RESTORATION

At Tennis Line Sol d.o.o., our restoration services rejuvenate aging and worn sports facilities, bringing them back to their prime condition. With extensive experience across Europe, we specialize in refurbishing tennis and pickleball courts, ensuring each surface meets professional standards and provides optimal playability. Whether it's resurfacing hard courts, renovating clay courts, or upgrading facilities with state-of-the-art materials, our skilled team ensures precision and quality in every project. Trust us to extend the life of your sports courts and enhance the playing experience for athletes and enthusiasts alike.

PRODUCTION OF TENNIS CLAY

Tennis Line Sol d.o.o. is a renowned leader in the production of high-quality tennis clay, catering to diverse court requirements across Europe. Our production line offers a variety of clay types, each tailored to enhance playing surfaces from amateur backyards to professional tournament standards. We proudly supply clay to notable figures in the industry, including Lubomir Schmidt, one of Europe's leading distributors. Our commitment to innovation and quality ensures that each batch of tennis clay supports optimal playability and durability, solidifying our position as a trusted provider in the sports construction market.
