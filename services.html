CONSTRUCTION

Boston Tennis & Sports Court Company (BTSCC) brings over 50 years of a stellar reputation for quality workmanship and outstanding service to your home or club. Our people have built and maintained among the most prestigious clubs and residences throughout Boston, the South Shore, Cape Cod, and the Islands. The history of the original company goes back to 1968 when the founders Dave Marsden and Bruce Mahler began Boston Tennis Court Construction Inc. Our full range of services include court construction, maintenance, reconditioning, resurfacing, repairs, and consultation. We also offer a complete line of nets, windscreens, ball machines, court lighting, as well as court maintenance tools and accessories.

Pickleball was invented in the mid-1960s by Joel Pritchard, a former congressman from Washington state, and two of his friends. The sport is named after Pritchard's dog, Pickles, who would chase the ball around during their games.

In the 1970s, the sport began to gain popularity in the United States and Canada, and the first official pickleball tournament was held in Seattle, Washington in 1984. Since then, the sport has continued to grow in popularity, with the formation of the USA Pickleball Association (USAPA) in 2005, which is the governing body for the sport in the United States. Today, pickleball is played in all 50 states and in many countries around the world.
