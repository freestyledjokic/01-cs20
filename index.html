ABOUT US

Tennis Line Sol d.o.o. is a company specializing in the design and construction of tennis courts, the production and sale of clay used in tennis courts, and the production and sale of tennis and other sports equipment. In addition to these activities, our company also deals with the automatic irrigation of sports fields and horticultural irrigation, including the design and sale of Rain Bird brand equipment. Tennis Line Sol d.o.o. began building tennis courts 25 years ago, and concurrently started the production and sale of tennis clay. Our company has constructed numerous tennis centers where professional tournaments are held, and the excellent reviews from supervisors at these tournaments have confirmed the quality we offer. Years of experience in the industry, the quality of our construction work, and the sale of top-quality tennis clay have positioned us as leaders in the BiH market and beyond.
